BIO

About Me

I am an astronomer and a PhD student in physics. My research combines theoretical modeling and numerical simulations to understand accretion processes in planet-forming regions.

I work with high-performance computing (HPC) environments, developing pipelines in Python and C++ that process the evolution of instabilities in accretion disks. I am also interested in astrochemical modeling of molecular clouds and circumstellar material.
